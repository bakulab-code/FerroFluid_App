About the app

A tactile magnetic liquid experience

FerroFluid is an interactive visual ASMR app built around responsive liquid surfaces, magnetic spikes, drawing, and ambient scenes.

Field

Touch the surface and place point or ring magnets to shape the fluid in real time.

Trace

Draw fading magnetic strokes that bloom into detailed ferrofluid spikes.

Drift

Watch evolving ambient scenes with automatic camera motion and built-in music.
